Minimize All Windows is a shortcut-driven macOS hack for System. Minimizes ALL windows of the current app. (Hold Shift to see it in slow motion!). In isolation that may sound small, but this kind of action is exactly the sort of thing that shapes how smooth or clumsy a normal day on a Mac feels.
