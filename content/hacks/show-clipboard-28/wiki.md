## What this hack does

Show Clipboard is a ui-driven macOS hack for Finder. Opens a window showing exactly what is currently copied. In isolation that may sound small, but this kind of action is exactly the sort of thing that shapes how smooth or clumsy a normal day on a Mac feels.

## Interaction to remember

The important interaction is: Finder > Edit > Show Clipboard. Keeping that phrasing in mind makes the trick easier to find again later.

## When to use it

- Use it when you are moving text between notes, chat, mail, and documents without breaking your flow.
- Use it when you are duplicating files or snippets before making edits.
- Use it when you are collecting research from multiple windows into one place.
