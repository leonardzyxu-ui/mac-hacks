## What this hack does

Status Bar is a settings-driven macOS hack for Finder. Displays item count and remaining disk space at the bottom. In isolation that may sound small, but this kind of action is exactly the sort of thing that shapes how smooth or clumsy a normal day on a Mac feels.

This hack is tagged Medium and low risk. The risk here is generally low, which shifts the emphasis away from safety and toward consistency. ## Where to find it

The most relevant settings path is `Finder > View > Show Status Bar`. If that exact label moves in a future macOS version, use System Settings search and look for the same feature name instead of assuming the hack disappeared.
